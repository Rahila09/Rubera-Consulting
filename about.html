Our Story

Who We Are

A multi-disciplinary team of innovative experts delivering engineering excellence for the Maritime and Offshore Industry.

Our Identity

Engineering Excellence, Delivered

Rubera Consulting Limited is a multi-disciplinary team of innovative and dedicated experts driven by talent, integrity, and aspiration to provide sustainable solutions and services in Engineering, Infrastructure, and Operations for the Maritime and Offshore Industry.

As inventive thinkers and expert collaborators, we combine our expertise, offering bespoke service tailored to fit project requirements and help our clients discover efficient and cost-effective ways of achieving their set goals.

Headquartered at 23 Platinum Way (Jakande), Lekki–Epe Expressway, Lagos — we serve clients across Nigeria and the broader West African maritime sector.
